Hello!

Rainbow Artist Alliance is a collective, a co-op, and a conversation for artists of the LGBTQ community. The goal of Rainbow Artist Alliance is to provide a collaborate platform and networking experience for LGBTQ artists with the hope of giving marginalized individuals a voice. Members of RAA will get their own profile on the website where they can promote their work, contribute to the blog, and share ideas on our discussion boards. Eventually RAA will coordinate events from coast to coast such as art shows, open mic nights, and poetry readings.

Ultimately, with enough funding, RAA will be made an official nonprofit cooperative that will give scholarships and grants to exceptional members of the alliance.
